ABOUT

Lalisa Manobal, known as Lisa, was born on March 27, 1997, in Thailand. She showed a passion for dancing and singing from a young age, which led her to pursue a career in entertainment. Lisa auditioned for YG Entertainment in Thailand and impressed with her dance skills and charisma.

After joining YG Entertainment, Lisa moved to South Korea to train further and eventually debuted as a member of the girl group BLACKPINK in 2016. Her unique style, powerful dance moves, and versatility as a rapper and singer quickly gained her recognition worldwide.

Throughout her career, Lisa has become a global icon, influencing fashion trends and inspiring fans with her talent and dedication.
