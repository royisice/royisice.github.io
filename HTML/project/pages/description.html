Biography

Lalisa Manobal, known mononymously as Lisa, is a Thai rapper, singer, and dancer based in South Korea. Born on March 27, 1997, in Buriram Province, Thailand, Lisa showed early talent in dance and music. She gained global recognition as a member of the South Korean girl group BLACKPINK, which debuted under YG Entertainment in 2016.

Lisa's journey in the music industry has been marked by her exceptional dance skills, charismatic stage presence, and versatile rap and vocal abilities. Beyond her music career, she has become a prominent figure in fashion and beauty, collaborating with major brands and setting trends worldwide.

Career Highlights

Lisa made her debut with BLACKPINK in August 2016, releasing the chart-topping singles "Whistle" and "Boombayah." The group's international success led to numerous achievements, including performances at major music festivals and collaborations with renowned artists.

In September 2021, Lisa ventured into her solo career with the release of her single album "LALISA." The title track broke records on various platforms, solidifying her status as a solo artist. She followed this success with the release of "Money," further showcasing her artistic range and dominance in the music scene.

Achievements

Lisa has received numerous awards for her contributions to music and fashion. She continues to inspire millions of fans worldwide through her music, performances, and influence on popular culture.
